FAQ

Is Trust powered by AI?

No. Trust is built by humans — students, educators, and researchers who care about source quality and clarity.

How do you decide what's trustworthy?

We look at source reliability, factual consistency, author background, and cross-checks with academic and journalistic standards.

Can I submit a source for review?

Yes! Just send it to contact@blacklink.net and our team will review it manually.
