Blacklink Trust Help

Your guide to using the most reliable source search platform.

Getting Started

Blacklink Trust helps you quickly find trustworthy sources with real-time analysis and scoring. To begin, simply enter a search term in the homepage search bar and press Enter.

Source Scoring

Each result is assigned a trust score based on Blacklink’s review algorithms. Green means highly trusted, yellow means mixed reliability, and red means untrustworthy or unverified.

Filters

Use the filters panel to narrow your search by category, publication date, and trust score. This ensures you’re only seeing the most relevant and reliable results.

Troubleshooting

  • No results? Try a different keyword or check your internet connection.
  • Site loading slowly? Blacklink Trust is optimized for low bandwidth, but occasional delays may occur.
  • Found an error? Click the “Report” button beside any result to send us feedback.

Contact Support

If you need more help, email us at support@blacklink.net.
