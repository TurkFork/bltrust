Trust Privacy Policy

Effective Date:September 23rd, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Blacklink Education (“Blacklink”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use Trust, our verified link and redirect system.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit a link through Trust, we automatically log:

  • The URL you visited
  • The date and time of the visit
  • Your IP address
  • Your Blacklink account ID (if signed in)
  • The number of times the link has been visited by your account or IP

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information to provide analytics, ensure link safety, prevent abuse, and improve the Trust system. We may also use aggregate data to understand how Trust is being used.

3. Data Storage

Logged data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore under the collection trust-redirect_logs. Data is retained for operational, analytical, and security purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data may be shared only:

  • With service providers who help operate Trust
  • As required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or security of users and systems

5. Your Choices

You may choose not to sign in with a Blacklink account, in which case only your IP-based visit data will be logged. Some features may require an account to function properly.

6. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of Trust constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
